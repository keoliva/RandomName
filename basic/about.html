Syllawise is an app designed to help students keep track of important due dates on their syllabi. Important assignments and exams can now be directly exported onto a calendar that will automatically send reminder emails and notifications to the student.


Follow these 3 simple steps to get started:

  Step 1: Upload Syllabus

  Step 2: Confirm dates and their corresponding assignments/exams

  Step 3: Provide an email address

And you're done!

Syllawise helps you be more organized than ever before!
